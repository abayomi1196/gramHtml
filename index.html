How long do drugs stay in your body?

Grams works by calculating the half-life elimination timeline of ingested drugs. Some drugs (notably alcohol and THC) cannot be easily modeled in this way, but we will release specialized calculators soon. Drug metabolism depends on ingester characteristics. For example, caffeine's half-life is 97 hours in infants but only 5 hours in adults. Research drugs before you consume them. This site is not medical advice.
